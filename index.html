Ozobot Calibration Circle

Place Ozobot inside the black circle before you start.

  1. Press and hold the power button for 5 seconds (or until the top LED flashes white), then let go.
  2. Ozobot will wiggle left and right and move out of the circle.
  3. If it blinks green, calibration worked.
  4. If it blinks red, start over and try again.
